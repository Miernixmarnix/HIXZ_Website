About Marnix

Your guide to Aruba's best-kept secrets.

The HIXZ Mission

Welcome! I specialize in making hiking and swimming adventures in Aruba fun, safe, and unforgettable for everyone. While most visitors stick to the crowded hotel beaches, I built HIXZ to show you the wild, rugged, and truly majestic side of the island that most people never get to see.

Whether we are navigating the volcanic steps of Hooiberg, exploring the hidden Natural Pool, or trekking through the Sasariwichi Dunes, my goal is to guide you step-by-step through the best nature Aruba has to offer.

Adventure Filming Packages

You focus on the hike, I'll focus on the camera. I offer professional filming options to capture your entire adventure using a DJI Osmo Action 5 camera paired with professional DJI microphones for crystal-clear audio. Whether we are trekking through the desert or swimming at the Natural Pool, my specialized underwater housing ensures we don't miss a single moment above or below the surface.

After the hike, you will receive fully edited, high-quality video clips—expertly cut and color-graded—perfectly formatted for your TikTok or Instagram feeds. You get to leave the island with an amazing experience and the exact content you need to remember it by.
