Natural Bridge & Blackstone Beach

Explore the rugged north coast where the ocean has carved spectacular formations out of coral rock. The trail to Blackstone Beach is a hidden gem for those looking to see Aruba's wilder side.

Arikok National Park

Nearly 20% of the island is a protected sanctuary. From the Spooky Trail's mysterious atmosphere to the peak of Jamanota, Arikok offers the most diverse hiking trails on the island.

Ayo & Casibari Rock Formations

Wander through massive monolithic boulders and ancient Arawak petroglyphs. These sites offer paved paths that make for an easy but mystical walk through Aruba's inland history.
